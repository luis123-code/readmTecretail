#### Cómo configurar

##### Cambiar nombre de un estado

<div style="background:#F5F5F5;border:1px solid #E5E5E5;border-radius:0.75rem;padding:1rem;margin-bottom:0.75rem;">
<div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:0.5rem;">
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">01</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">En la pestaña <span style="font-weight:600;color:#121212;">Estado Interno</span>, busca el campo de nombre</span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">02</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Escribe el nombre personalizado (ej: "Etapa", "Vendedor", "Zona")</span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#A7F3D0;color:#065F46;flex-shrink:0;">03</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">El nombre se guarda automáticamente</span></div>
</div>
</div>

##### Agregar una nueva opción de estado

<div style="background:#F5F5F5;border:1px solid #E5E5E5;border-radius:0.75rem;padding:1rem;margin-bottom:0.75rem;">
<div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:0.5rem;">
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">01</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Haz clic en el botón <span style="font-weight:600;color:#121212;">"+ Agregar opción"</span></span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">02</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Escribe el nombre de la opción (ej: "Pendiente", "En Proceso", "Completado")</span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">03</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Selecciona un color para identificar visualmente el estado</span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">04</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Haz clic en <span style="font-weight:600;color:#121212;">Guardar</span></span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#A7F3D0;color:#065F46;flex-shrink:0;">05</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">La opción aparecerá en la lista</span></div>
</div>
</div>

##### Editar una opción existente

<div style="background:#F5F5F5;border:1px solid #E5E5E5;border-radius:0.75rem;padding:1rem;margin-bottom:0.75rem;">
<div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:0.5rem;">
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">01</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Busca la opción que quieres editar</span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">02</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Haz clic en el icono de editar (lápiz)</span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">03</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Modifica el nombre y/o color</span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">04</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Haz clic en <span style="font-weight:600;color:#121212;">Guardar</span></span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#A7F3D0;color:#065F46;flex-shrink:0;">05</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Los cambios se aplicarán a todas las ventas con ese estado</span></div>
</div>
</div>

##### Eliminar una opción

<div style="background:#F5F5F5;border:1px solid #E5E5E5;border-radius:0.75rem;padding:1rem;">

<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">01</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Busca la opción que quieres eliminar</span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#E5E5E5;color:#6B6B6B;flex-shrink:0;">02</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Haz clic en el icono de eliminar (basura)</span></div>
<div style="display:flex;gap:0.75rem;align-items:flex-start;"><span style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:10px;padding:0.1rem 0.35rem;border-radius:4px;background:#A7F3D0;color:#065F46;flex-shrink:0;">03</span><span style="font-size:12px;color:#6B6B6B;line-height:1.5;">Confirma la eliminación.
